# Calculator

A mathematical expression calculator with plugin support.

## Examples

```
to exit, enter the q
to show the list of available functions, enter the f

enter the expression: (deg(180^--1) ^ 2) ^0.5
deg(180.00 ^ --1.00) ^ 2.00 ^ 0.5000 = 3.14159

enter the expression: cos(deg(180))
cos(deg(180.00)) = -1

enter the expression: 3 + 2  4^2 + 1
3.00 + 2.00 = 5
4.00 ^ 2.00 + 1.00 = 17

enter the expression: sin(cos(3.14/2))
sin(cos(3.14 / 2.00)) = 0.000796327

enter the expression: 2^--2
2.00 ^ --2.00 = 4

enter the expression: -2^-2
-2.00 ^ -2.00 = -0.25

enter the expression: 3*3*3 + 3^1
3.00 * 3.00 * 3.00 + 3.00 ^ 1.00 = 30
```
